1. **Copy the entire prompt** (including the triple backticks)
2. **Paste into Gemini** image generation
3. **Generate the image**
4. **Download as PNG or JPG**
5. **Save with numbered filename** (e.g., `01-frustrated-person.png`)
6. **Import into your video editor** (Premiere Pro, Final Cut, DaVinci Resolve, CapCut, etc.)

### File Naming Convention:

- `02-phone-screen.png`
- `03-clock-time-passing.png`
- ... and so on
